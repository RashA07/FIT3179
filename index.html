Global CO2 Emissions

Large countries may have relatively low CO2 emissions and vice versa. Clearly many factors cause these disparities.

Emissions in relation to Population & GDP

While population is directly proportional to the emissions of a country, that is not the case with regards to GDP. It is clear that some developed countries with high GDP have taken steps to reduce their emissions.

CO2 Emissions over the years grouped by Region

We observe a rise in emissions in all countries globally. However, several have taken positive steps and slowed that growth in the recent past.

CO2 Emissions caused by Global Shipping and Aviation

Although frequently brought up in world media, global shipping and aviation make up a mere 1.83% and 1.47% of global emissions. They have nonetheless seen significant growth over the years.
